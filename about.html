Meet Hot Water Solutions

Est 2000

Our Story

  Hot Water Solutions, established in 2000 in Houston, Texas, was born with a clear mission: to cater to the needs of trade and corporate accounts across North America, spanning the Plumbing, HVAC Builders, and industrial sectors. Our vision was to create an expansive network of highly skilled trade professionals, facilitating seamless access to wholesalers.

  In our eyes, everyone within the commercial, residential, and industrial supply chains is considered a valued customer. Our client base ranges from manufacturers and wholesalers to contractors and engineers, all of whom benefit from our unwavering commitment to meet their unique requirements.

Our Comprehensive Services

We extend dedicated support to our contractor customers, leveraging our field expertise to ensure flawless installations. Moreover, we assist product managers and estimators in their endeavors, providing tailored solutions.

Technical Expertise

Quality Assurance
